Fluncle is one selector, no team, digging drum & bass since '90 and logging every certified banger as a **finding** with its own permanent coordinate. The whole archive is **Fluncle's Findings**, kept at [fluncle.com](/).

These docs are the field manual for the machinery, not the music. The music lives in the [findings](/); this is how you reach it from a terminal, a script, an agent, or a DNS resolver.

## One archive, many doors

Every surface here is a thin client of the same public API. None of them owns the data; they're all reading the same findings, just dressed for a different room. That's the point: the archive is the page, wherever you reach it from.

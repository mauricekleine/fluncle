The findings come to you, in whatever format your reader speaks. Three feeds carry **Fluncle's Findings**; a fourth carries the mixtapes as a podcast.

## Fluncle's Findings

The same archive, three ways. Point your reader at whichever it likes — they all carry every finding, newest first.

| Feed          | URL                                 | Type                    |
| ------------- | ----------------------------------- | ----------------------- |
| **RSS**       | `https://www.fluncle.com/rss.xml`   | `application/rss+xml`   |
| **JSON Feed** | `https://www.fluncle.com/feed.json` | `application/feed+json` |
| **Atom**      | `https://www.fluncle.com/atom.xml`  | `application/atom+xml`  |

Each item is a finding: the banger, the artist and title, the [Log ID](/docs/log-id), the day it was found, and the link to its log page.

## The mixtape podcast

A **mixtape** is Fluncle dreaming — many findings consolidated into one long recording, a checkpoint closing a chapter. They ship as a podcast:

```
https://www.fluncle.com/podcast.xml
```

Subscribe in any podcast app and the mixtapes arrive as episodes.

## The mothership

If you'd rather have it land in your inbox, board the mothership: fresh bangers, every Friday, from Fluncle. Subscribe from the [home page](/), the [rave terminal](/docs/ssh), or the [MCP server](/docs/mcp)'s `subscribe_newsletter` tool.
